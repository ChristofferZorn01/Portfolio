What I've done
Student Project

Tilt and the Induction of Cybersickness in CVR

This study tries to establish certain thresholds and criteria for camera handlings – with focus on tilt – to reduce the risk of inducing CS in CVR. This study did not aim to limit filmmakers to only make CVR videos a certain way, but simply provide tools and methods to give their audience the best experience with minimum to no CS.

Student Project

Sofware

This project revolved around making an immersive music video inside a virtual reality environment. The thesis about this project was about whether or not the virtual reality experience enhanced users feeling of being immersed.

Student Project

Recorder Hero

Based on the theme “Adaptive instrument learning system”, this paper explores how a system with an augmented recorder can enhance the learning experience for beginners, compared to traditional teaching methods. To aid the learning experience, a physically augmented recorder and an appurtenant computer application has been developed and evaluated on young students, by precisely tracking their performance.

Student Project

Optical Flow and The Art of Dancing

This study examines the embodiment in interactive installations and investigates how movement as the natural medium of self-expression of a dance choreographer, can be combined with an interactive art installation to elicit curiosity, ludic engagement and inspiration. Based on an analysis covering important subjects such as; interaction and installation art, perceiving both objects and motion, and motion capture with optical flow an installation prototype was implemented through the use of the programming software Processing.

Student Project

Product Layout

This study examines the relaxing and restorative possibilities of a virtual restorative environment combined with practices of receptive music therapy. Based on an analysis covering subjects like; well-being, restorative environments, virtual reality, and music theory, a virtual environment prototype was implemented in the Unity 3D engine. The prototype was tested with defined restorative components as well as usability and its influence on the holistic experience as the main testing and evaluation aspect.
